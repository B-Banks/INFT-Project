Having trouble picking classes?


Deciding on what to take each year is difficult! Even figuring out what classes are available can be daunting. This tool is here to help you explore your options, ensure that graduation requirements are met, and chart a path to a successful high school experience!

Graduation Requirements

Learn about the requirements The Arkansas Department of Education and Bentonville Public Schools have for graduation. As well as how to earn BPS' special Honors and Career Studies Completer statuses.

Explore Courses

Bentonville West offers a wide variety of courses across an array of subjects. Take a minute to explore all of the options available to you.

Example Paths

Overwhelmed with the possibilites and need a starting point? We have a set of example paths you could take. One is sure to match your interests. Use it as a starting point and customize it from there to make the perfect plan for you.

Plan your Experience

Use this tool to map out your BWHS experience. Input the credits you've already earned in past years and add in the classes you'd like to take in the future to see if your plan meets course prerequsite and graduation requirements.
